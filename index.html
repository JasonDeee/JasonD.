    So, Who am i?

    - "I'm Batman"

    JasonD.

    Viet Cao

    I was born in Phu Ly city, 2000. My Parents gifted me the name

    That very much inspired of a famous poet Nam Cao.

    But friends call me Jason, which after my music idol

    - Jason Derulo - And You can call me too!

    2018, I started learning at PTIT as a Designer

    And as an Account Planner. For the present, I am wanting to

    Provide one-stop support for Videography, Animation, CGI,

    Landing Page Design, and FrontEnd Coding as an Intern.

    'I love Design, Technology and the Story'

    And also love the Batman.

    To me, I consider designing itself just like a SupperHero's Cape.
    If products or services just like the Batmans that help everyone
    Or every client solving their problems, it is the role of design
    To act as an intermediary for users
    To visualize what the Hero is capable of.
    We strive every day to create a "Cape" that
    Perfectly matches between
    Users and our Batman.

    DESIGN

    In order to against the crime, the Batman
    Always has to renew or Upgrade his own Gears, his BatMobile,
    or his BatCape. So does the Technology, it changes everyday.
    Isaac Asimov said: "I do not fear computers. I fear lack of them."
    So, I want to be a person who can always catch up
    With the latest technology and respond to changes.
    I suppose it is the duty of designers
    As well as engineers to promote innovation.

    Once, My college dean told Learners about PublicSpeaking:
    "The best way to initiate a topic is telling a story.
    Everyone like following the stories".
    I really love the way a story can easily draw people's attention.
    Moreover, i love the way they simplify things
    And make them easier to be kept in mind.
    I believe that success peoples
    Are the success Storytellers.
